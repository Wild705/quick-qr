Privacy Policy — Quick QR: QR Code Generator & Scanner

Last updated: May 2026

Data Collection

Quick QR does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect browsing history
  • We do not track your activity
  • We do not send data to external servers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking scripts
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not share any information with third parties

Local Storage

Quick QR stores your QR code history locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local). This data never leaves your browser and is only accessible by the extension itself. You can clear this data at any time by removing the extension.

Permissions

activeTab: Used only to read the current page URL when you click the extension icon, so it can pre-fill the URL field for QR code generation.

storage: Used to save your QR code history locally on your device.

Changes

If we ever update this privacy policy, changes will be posted on this page.

Contact
